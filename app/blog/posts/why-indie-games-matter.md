# The Soul of Independent Gaming

Indie games are the **experimental laboratory** of the software world. They're where new mechanics are born and where storytelling takes its most daring risks.

## Creative Freedom vs. Corporate Rigidity

Unlike AAA titles, indie games are often the work of small, highly focused teams—sometimes just one person. This allows for:
- **Pure Expression:** No committees, just raw vision.
- **Innovation:** Trying things that would be too risky for a multi-million dollar budget.
- **Connection:** A more personal relationship between developer and player.
